Q: Switch between Egoperspective and Turntable mode

Egoperspective

Controls Action
W: Move forward.
A: Move left.
S: Move backward.
D: Move right.
Mouse-Click and Drag: Look around.

Turntable

Controls Action
Mouse-Click and Drag: rotate the room.
C: Switch between dragging and rotating objects.
Mouse-Click (in Drag-Mode): Select an object, then move your mouse to find the preferred spot and click again to confirm placement.
To move an object one needs to move the cursor along the bottom plane to place it.
The Ceiling_lamp can also be placed via the ceiling.
Mouse-Click (in Rotation-Mode): Select an object, then move your mouse to rotate it and click again to confirm the rotation.
X (in Drag- or Rotation-Controls): An object needs to be selected in either Drag- or Rotation-Controls.
When the x-key is pressed the object is deleted.
